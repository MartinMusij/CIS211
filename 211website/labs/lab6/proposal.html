Argentina
Land of Natural Wonder

Topic

This site will be about Argentina, a little bit of the culture, the food, and the landscape. I think Argentina is often overlooked as culturally rich country and would like to do it justice.

Site Goals (Purpose)

  • To be able to shed a light on the large amount of European influence in certain parts of the country
  • Highlight the importance that the culture has on the economy (soccer, food, etc.)
  • Unfortunately, briefly explain how corrupt the government is
  • Show some premium vacation destinations that do not suffer form the infamous insecurity that hovers around the capital city

Audience

People who enjoy a culturaly varied country and would like to travel to a decently affordable locationr

Client

English speaking people who would like to enjoy a vacation to a relatively affordable vacation with lots to see and do.
